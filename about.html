About Us

Welcome to Maa Foundaton Since 2019

Welcome you to the Maa Foundation.
Our foundation is located in the village of Duburi,Jajapur Road,Odisha.This foundation was first lunched in 2019.for the first time it started with seven orphans.Over time the foundation extened its branches to Nayaghar,Kandhamal and Boudh area.In this places foundation now takes 241 number of orphans.
The MAA FOUNDATION gives them good hygenic food & good clothes for their necessery. The foundation provied them best education and other extracurricular activites

Our foundation members go to one by one villages every Saturday to give free health check-ups to the poorest children there.And we provide them with free medicine where necessary.Because our goal is to keep every child healthy and happy

The members of Maa foundation have clean out The Puri,Chandrabhaga & Gopalpur beaches in phases.And requesting the locals to keep the beach clean every time.

Some of the benefactors who helped our orohaned the orphanage in this month.And has extended a hand for the betterment of the foundation.

We have hired highly educated teachers and educators to educate our orphans.Those who teach orphans & poor children at the foundation as well as social education.
